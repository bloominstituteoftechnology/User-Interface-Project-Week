    Milestones

    • 25 DEC 1980

      S&J Architects was found by Mr. Smith and Mr. Jones

    • 13 JUL 1985

      S&J Architects is chosen as the fastest growing startup by Arch Magazine.

    • 5 OCT 1990

      The company goes public with their first IPO, having shown tremendous growth in the first decade of their existence there was an overwhelming response in the market.

    • 27 FEB 1993

      The company expands world wide and runs projects in 10 major cities all around the world.

    • 17 APR 1996

      The company achieves a historical feat by desigining a building that appears to defy gravity. Designers all over the world call it "The Levitator".

    • 25 DEC 2000

      S&J Architects complete 20 years with a work force of 10,000 employees. The company starts its philanthropic activities on a global scale and chooses to educate children all around the world.

    • 31 OCT 2005

      Another breakthrough in innovation for the company when it invents the rotating building which can change its orientation with a flick of a button.

    • 19 JUL 2018

      28 years in existence now, S&J Architects becomes the global leader in interior design. Arch Magazine honors the achievements in an International Event.
